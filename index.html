Pathfinder Conditions Summary

In Pathfinder, conditions are ongoing effects that alter a character's statistics or capabilities. Some conditions have values that represent their severity; if affected by multiple sources of the same valued condition, apply only the highest value.

Condition Effect(s) per Level / Value Duration and Removal
Blinded You cannot see. All normal terrain is difficult terrain. You cannot detect using vision and automatically critically fail Perception checks requiring sight. You take a –4 status penalty to Perception if vision is your only precise sense. Immune to visual effects. Ends when the effect expires or is removed by magic such as sound body.
Broken (Objects only) HP reduced below Broken Threshold. Armor still grants its item bonus but gives a status penalty to AC (–1 light, –2 medium, –3 heavy). Other items cannot be used for their normal function. Ends when repaired above its Broken Threshold using the Repair action or spells such as mending.
Clumsy Status penalty equal to the value to Dexterity-based checks and DCs (AC, Reflex saves, ranged attacks, Acrobatics, Stealth, and Thievery). Ends when the duration expires. Can be counteracted by surging serum or sure footing.
Concealed Creatures must succeed at a DC 5 flat check to target you with a Strike or effect. Ends when the obscuring effect ends or you leave the area.
Confused You are off-guard, cannot use reactions, and must spend all actions attacking or casting offensive spells against the nearest creature. Ends when the duration expires. You may attempt a DC 11 flat check whenever you take damage.
Dazzled Everything is concealed to you. You must succeed at a DC 5 flat check to target creatures or objects. Ends when the duration expires or through magic such as sound body.
Deafened You cannot hear. You take a –2 status penalty to Perception and initiative. Auditory actions require a DC 5 flat check. Ends when the duration expires or through magic such as sound body.
Doomed Your maximum dying value is reduced by the doomed value. If reduced to 0, you die instantly. Value decreases by 1 after a full night's rest. Removed entirely upon death.
Drained Status penalty equal to the value on Constitution-based checks. Lose HP equal to Level × Value; maximum HP is reduced by the same amount. Value decreases by 1 after a full night's rest. Maximum HP returns as the value decreases.
Dying You are unconscious. At value 4 (or lower if doomed), you die. Make recovery checks each turn. Ends when the value reaches 0 or healing restores HP.
Encumbered You are clumsy 1 and take a –10-foot status penalty to all Speeds. Ends when excess Bulk is removed.
Enfeebled Status penalty equal to the value on Strength-based checks and DCs (attack rolls, damage rolls, Athletics). Ends when the duration expires. Can be counteracted by surging serum or sound body.
Fatigued –1 status penalty to AC and saving throws. Cannot use exploration activities while traveling. Ends after a full night's rest.
Fleeing Must spend all actions moving away from the source. Cannot Delay or Ready. Ends when the effect expires.
Frightened Status penalty equal to the value to all checks and DCs. Value decreases by 1 at the end of each of your turns.
Grabbed You are off-guard and immobilized. Manipulate actions require a DC 5 flat check. Ends when the grabber releases you, is incapacitated, the effect expires, or you Escape.
Immobilized You cannot use move actions. Ends when the duration expires or by Escaping.
Off-Guard –2 circumstance penalty to AC. Ends when the source of the condition ends.
Paralyzed You are off-guard and cannot act except for purely mental actions. Ends when the duration expires. Can be removed by sure footing.
Persistent Damage Take the listed damage at the end of each of your turns. After damage, attempt a DC 15 flat check (DC 10 with effective assistance).
Petrified Turned to stone. You become an object, cannot act, and cannot sense anything. Ends when the duration expires or through appropriate magic.
Prone You are lying down. You are off-guard, take a –2 circumstance penalty to attack rolls, and can only Crawl. Ends when you use the Stand action.
Sickened Status penalty equal to the value on all checks and DCs. Cannot willingly eat or drink. Spend 1 action to Retch, attempting a Fortitude save to reduce the value.
Slowed Lose a number of actions each turn equal to the value. Ends when the effect expires.
Stunned You cannot act. Value-based stunned removes actions until the value reaches 0. Ends when the duration expires or the value reaches 0.
Stupefied Status penalty equal to the value on Intelligence-, Wisdom-, and Charisma-based checks and DCs. Spellcasting requires a flat check (DC 5 + value). Ends when the duration expires or through magic such as sound body.
Unconscious –4 status penalty to AC, Reflex, and Perception. You are off-guard, blinded, prone, and cannot act. Ends if you take damage, an ally wakes you with Interact, or after appropriate rest.
Wounded If you gain the dying condition, your dying value begins at 0 + wounded value. Ends when restored to full HP and resting for 10 minutes, or via a successful Treat Wounds.
